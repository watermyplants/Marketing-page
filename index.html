Water My Plants

The water reminder app for your plants

It’s easy to forget to water your plants, but it doesn’t have to be. Happy Plant reminds you to water plants through game-like notifications, plant-selfies, and time-lapse videos so you can watch your Happy Plants grow baby grow.
